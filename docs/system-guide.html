1. System Overview

CrosCal Fitness is a web-based gym management system that handles member registration, memberships, attendance (QR-based check-in), coach-client assignments, workout & meal plans, payments, and analytics. It is a Next.js application that runs on http://localhost:3000 in development.

Key Features

  • Role-based dashboards for Admin, Coach, and Client
  • Admin approval workflow for new client registrations
  • Membership plans (Basic / Premium) and renewals
  • QR-code attendance: client shows QR, coach/admin scans
  • Workout library, goals tracking, and meal plans
  • Coach assignment per client, with personalised plans
  • Real-time analytics for revenue and attendance

2. Roles & Permissions

Admin

The Owner

Approves accounts and runs the gym.

  • Approve / reject sign-ups
  • Manage memberships & pricing
  • Assign coaches to clients
  • Record payments & renewals
  • Manage workouts & equipment
  • View revenue / attendance analytics
Coach

The Trainer

Guides their assigned clients.

  • See assigned clients
  • Create / manage session schedules
  • Set workout & nutrition plans
  • Scan client QR to check them in
  • Track client progress
Client

The Member

Trains and tracks progress.

  • Buy / renew membership
  • Check in via personal QR
  • View workouts & meal plan
  • Track goals & attendance
  • View payment history

3. End-to-End Navigation Flow

The diagram below shows the full path users take through CrosCal — from the very first visit on / to logging out. Routes are protected by middleware: a user can only enter the area that matches their role.

A. Entry & Authentication

Visit / → Redirect to /auth/sign-in → Sign-in form
New user? → /auth/register → Awaits admin approval → Sign in

B. Role-Based Routing (after login)

Login success → ADMIN → /admin/analytics
Login success → COACH → /coach/dashboard
Login success → CLIENT → /client/dashboard

C. Client Day-to-Day Flow

Dashboard → Buy Membership → Pay → Admin approves payment → Access unlocked
Show QR at gym → Coach/Admin scans → Attendance logged → Workout / Goals

D. Coach Daily Flow

Dashboard → My Clients → Set plan / schedule → Scan client QR

E. Admin Operational Flow

Analytics → Approve Accounts → Sell Membership → Assign Coach → Record Payment → Renewals

F. Exit

Any page → Sidebar user menu → Log out → Back to /auth/sign-in

4. Getting Started (First-Time Setup)

Use this once to bring the system online on a fresh machine.

1Install dependencies
Open a terminal in the project folder and run npm install.
2Configure environment
Set DATABASE_URL and NEXTAUTH_SECRET in the .env file.
3Apply database schema
Run npm run migrate (uses Prisma). Then npm run generate.
4Seed demo data
Run npm run seed. This creates the admin / coach / client demo accounts.
5Start the server
Run npm run dev and open http://localhost:3000 in your browser.
6Sign in
You will be redirected to /auth/sign-in. Use the demo credentials in §1.

5. Step-by-Step: Client Workflow

This is the journey of a member from the moment they discover the gym to a fully active training routine.

5.1 Register an Account

1Open the sign-in page/auth/sign-in
Click “Register as a client” at the bottom of the form.
2Fill the registration form/auth/register
Enter name, email, password, plus optional personal info (phone, date of birth, gender, occupation, address pinned on the map, emergency contact, and fitness notes).
3Submit and wait for approval
The account is created with INACTIVE status. The admin must approve it before you can sign in. You'll see a green confirmation banner.

5.2 First Login & Buy a Membership

4Sign in/auth/sign-in
Use your registered email and password. You land on the Client Dashboard.
5View dashboard/client/dashboard
The dashboard shows: current membership, check-ins this month, goal progress, attendance chart, today's workout, and your personal QR code. With no membership yet, most cards show "—".
6Choose a membership plan/client/memberships
Use the sidebar → Memberships. Pick Basic or Premium (Premium includes a coach). Submit to request enrolment.
7Pay at the front desk/client/payments
Payment is recorded by the admin. Track its status under Payments. Once the admin marks payment completed, your membership becomes ACTIVE.

5.3 Daily Use — Check-in & Train

8Check in at the gym/client/dashboard
Scroll to the My QR code card. Show it to the coach/admin at the entrance. They scan it and your attendance is logged automatically. Alternatively, open Schedules, click a session, then press Check in.
9Follow today's workout/client/workouts
Use the Workout player on the dashboard or open the Workouts page to see your assigned routines step-by-step.
10Track goals/client/goals
Open Goals from the sidebar to update progress on assigned goals. Completed goals raise the "goals" counter on your dashboard.
11Follow the meal plan/client/meal-plan
If you have a coach (Premium), your daily calorie / protein targets and weekly nutrition advice appear here and on the dashboard's “Your coach's plan” card.
12View schedule & attendance/client/schedules · /client/attendance
See upcoming gym sessions in Schedules, and your historical check-ins under Attendance.
Note: Without an active membership, the sidebar hides Goals, Meal Plan, Schedules and Attendance. Buying or renewing a membership re-enables them.

6. Step-by-Step: Coach Workflow

1Sign in/auth/sign-in
Coach is auto-redirected to /coach/dashboard.
2Review the dashboard/coach/dashboard
See total assigned clients, today's sessions, plus sessions and attendance charts for the last 4 weeks.
3Open My Clients/coach/clients
Browse the clients assigned to you. Click a client to view profile, history, and set their plan (nutrition objective, daily calories, protein, weekly gym sessions, schedule notes).
4Manage schedules/coach/schedules
Create or edit upcoming sessions for your clients on the calendar. Each session appears on the client's Schedules page.
5Check a client in
On a session's details, open the QR scanner. Point the camera at the client's QR code (shown on their dashboard). Attendance is saved instantly.
6Log out
Sidebar → user avatar (bottom-left) → Log out.

7. Step-by-Step: Admin Workflow

7.1 Daily Operations

1Sign in as admin/auth/sign-in
Auto-redirects to /admin/analytics.
2Check analytics/admin/analytics
Review KPIs: Total Members, Active Memberships, Today's Attendance, and Revenue This Month, plus attendance (14 days) and revenue (6 months) charts.
3Approve new sign-ups/admin/accounts
Switch to the Pending tab. For each row, click the green checkmark to activate the account, or the ban icon to reject. You can also create or edit Admin / Coach accounts from this screen.
4Maintain membership plans/admin/memberships
Add, edit, or archive membership plans (name, type, price, duration). These plans appear to clients on their Memberships page.
5Sell a membership to a client/admin/client-memberships
Pick the client, pick a plan, set the start date. The client immediately sees the active membership on their dashboard.
6Assign a coach/admin/coach-assignments
For Premium members, attach a coach. This unlocks the “From your coach” card on the client dashboard and gives the coach access to that client.
7Record a payment/admin/payments
Add a payment for a client (cash / card / transfer) and mark it COMPLETED. The membership becomes active and revenue charts update.
8Handle renewals/admin/renewals
See memberships that are expiring soon. Process renewal & payment in one click.

7.2 Schedules & Attendance

9Open Schedules/admin/schedules
Master calendar of every coach session and class. Add, edit, reschedule, or cancel sessions.
10Open Attendance/admin/attendance
Live feed of all check-ins. Use the QR scanner here for walk-in check-ins.

7.3 Library & Inventory

11Manage workouts/admin/workouts
Create workout templates with exercises, sets and reps. Coaches assign these to clients.
12Manage goals/admin/goals · /admin/client-goals
Define reusable workout goals, then attach them to clients under Client Goals.
13Track equipment/admin/equipment
Add machines, weights and accessories with availability status.

9. Logout & Session Handling

  • Logout is in the bottom-left of every page: click your avatar → Log out.
  • You are returned to /auth/sign-in.
  • Sessions are JWT-based; sign out from one tab signs out all tabs once they refresh.
  • Visiting another role's URL while logged in (e.g. a coach opening /admin/*) immediately redirects to /auth/sign-in.

10. Troubleshooting

SymptomCauseFix
"Invalid email or password" after registering Account is still INACTIVE; admin has not approved you. Ask an admin to open /admin/accounts → Pending → Approve.
Goals / Meal plan / Schedules missing from client sidebar No active membership. Admin records a paid membership, or client purchases & admin confirms payment.
QR scan doesn't check in Client has no membership, or session belongs to a different coach. Verify membership is ACTIVE and the coach is assigned.
Admin can't see revenue No payments recorded with status COMPLETED. Open /admin/payments and mark payments completed.
Redirect loop to /auth/sign-in Session expired or wrong role for that URL. Sign in again with the correct role's credentials.
